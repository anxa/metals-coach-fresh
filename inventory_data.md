3. Combine inventory with price (this is where decisions form)

This is the most important part.

Scenario A: Inventories falling + price rising

Best case

Confirms trend

Forward expectation positive

Upside moves likely to persist

Action:

Do NOT fade strength

Scenario B: Inventories falling + price flat

Market storing energy
